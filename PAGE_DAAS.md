## SECTION 4 — How It Works

**Label:**
> Our Process

**Headline:**
> From Brief to Delivered.
> In 48 Hours.

**Steps:**

**01 — Subscribe & Onboard**
> Pick a plan. Tell us about your brand — your colors, fonts, tone, and what you're building. We set up your Notion board and Drive folder. You're ready to submit in under 24 hours.

**02 — Submit Your Request**
> Drop a brief anytime via your Notion board. Tell us what you need, attach your references, and we'll confirm it's in the queue. No email chains. No back-and-forth to get started.

**03 — We Design**
> Your dedicated designer gets to work. Most requests are delivered within 48 hours. Complex projects are broken into clear stages — you always know where things stand.

**04 — Review & Approve**
> We send the draft to your Drive folder. You review, leave feedback, and we revise — as many times as needed until it's right. When you're happy, you say the word. Done.

## SECTION 9 — FAQ

**Label:**
> Questions

**Headline:**
> Straight answers.

---

**Q: What exactly counts as "one design"?**
> One design = one asset, one concept, one primary size. A feed post and a story version of the same post = two designs. We'll always tell you upfront if a request spans multiple slots.

**Q: What if I need something that's not on the list?**
> Some work — like logos, mascots, and custom illustration — is available as a one-off add-on. We'll quote you a fixed price before anything starts. No surprises.

**Q: How does pausing work?**
> Your subscription runs on a 31-day billing cycle. If you pause on day 18, the remaining 13 days are saved. When you resume, those 13 days pick up exactly where you left off. Unused days from your design quota don't carry over — only billing days do.

**Q: What if I'm not happy with the output?**
> Revise until you are. Unlimited revisions are included in every plan — no caps, no extra charges. We iterate until the output is right.

**Q: How do I submit requests?**
> Via your Notion board (or WhatsApp for local clients). Every request has a brief template so nothing gets lost. Your designer gets a clean, complete brief every time.

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**
> Yes. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Q: Who will be designing my work?**
> A dedicated designer is assigned to your account — not a rotating pool. They stay with you, learn your brand, and get better at your briefs over time.

**Q: Do you work with international clients?**
> Yes. Global plans are priced in USD, communicate in English, and deliver via Figma + Notion. Payment via Stripe, PayPal, or Wise.
